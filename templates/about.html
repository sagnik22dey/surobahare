My Story

With a deep passion for music and teaching, I guide students to discover their unique voice while building confidence and stage presence. My approach focuses not only on correct pitch and technique but also on making learning enjoyable and meaningful.

Through Sur-O-Bahare, I aim to create a nurturing space where every child feels encouraged to express themselves through music — whether they are a complete beginner or have some experience already.

I believe that every child has a unique musical gift waiting to be discovered. My role as a mentor is to patiently guide, encourage, and celebrate every small step they take on this beautiful journey.

Teaching Approach

Five pillars that define every Sur-O-Bahare session

  • Personalized Attention

    Every student receives individual focus — no one-size-fits-all approach here.

  • Correct Pitch & Vocal Techniques

    Systematic training on voice control, breath support, and musical accuracy.

  • Confidence Through Performance

    Regular practice sessions and mini-performances help students overcome stage fear.

  • Fun & Engaging Environment

    Music should bring joy! Every session is designed to be enjoyable and motivating.

  • Self-Practice & Creativity

    Students are encouraged to practice independently and explore their creative instincts.
